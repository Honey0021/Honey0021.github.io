Suji ka Halwa

Ingredients Quantity
Suji 1/2 cup
sugar 1/3 cup
ghee 1/4 cup
water 1-1/2 cup
cardamon powder 1/8 tsp
sliced almonds for garnish 1 tbsp

Method

Step 1:Boil the water with sugar, and cardamom powder in a pan over low medium heat. Bring to a boil and Set aside.
Step 2: Melt the ghee in a frying pan on medium heat. Add sooji and roast to golden brown color; stirring constantly this will take about 5 to 8 minutes. Sooji will be golden brown in color and will have a light sweet aroma.
Step 3: Add the syrup slowly, as you add the syrup to sooji water will splatter. Turn down the heat to medium low and let it cook for about 2-3 minutes.
Step 4: Garnish with almonds.
